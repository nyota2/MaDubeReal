Who We Are

An African educational media company

MaDube Books develops engaging Curriculum Enrichment Resources designed to preserve, celebrate and promote African languages, folklore and cultural heritage. We create learning experiences that inspire children to read, think, create and explore while developing pride in their identity and culture.

We believe children should never have to choose between learning and enjoyment. The most powerful learning happens when children are engaged, inspired and able to see themselves reflected in the stories they read, the games they play and the activities they enjoy.

Our Mission

To create engaging, culturally authentic learning experiences that complement national curricula and inspire children to develop a lifelong love of learning.

Our Vision

To become Africa's trusted partner in developing Curriculum Enrichment Resources that bring African heritage to life.
